## Steps to follow
1. Start the recording.
2, Visite the test site: [Note: Do not visit the URL unless you're ready and recording]
3. Enter store password"bevy"
4.open the first product you see on this page.
5. Ignore the navagation bar and the footer. Your Area of interest should be everything else except the navigation and footer, and you must test everything throughly.
6. As you find each issue, talk about them on the video recording . Example: "I see the front size for the price differs from the Figma". You dont have to talk about it in detail.
7. End the recording within 10 minutes.
8. once finished, please copy the link to your clipboard and paste it into the blank box below. If you encounter any difficulties pasting the link. please visit this form(link -not avaliable) and submit the video link there.
9. Failure to follow the steps mentioned above will result in a disqualification.
